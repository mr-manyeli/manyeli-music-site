About The Artist:
Motaung (born Motaung Calvin Manyeli; 19 June 2005 in Rustenburg) is more than a name, it's a sound. A producer, composer and lyricist creating moments through music. Motaung builds from silence, shaping bars into truth and beats into feeling. A Rustenburg-born creator with a sound that speaks louder than words.
